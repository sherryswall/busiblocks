Due to TGA having a separate HR and Finance system to DoHA, TGA staff cannot register for DoHA in-house training courses displayed on the DoHA intranet.

TGA staff can register for TGA in-house training courses – many of which are TGA-tailored versions of the DoHA programs – by visiting the TGA Training Schedule at
